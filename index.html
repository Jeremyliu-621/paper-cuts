Controls

Player 1

  • A / D — move
  • W — jump (×3)
  • S — crouch / drop-through
  • F — attack
  • G — special
  • Left Shift — shield

Player 2

  • ◀ / ▶ — move
  • ▲ — jump (×3)
  • ▼ — crouch / drop-through
  • . — attack
  • / — special
  • Right Shift — shield

Attacks change with your state: in the air → hammer slam (spikes down, held till you land); with momentum (right after a dash, kept by jumping) → super punch 🥊. Special with momentum → ultra punch up close or a super shot 🚀 at range.

Momentum: a dash charges it (and a jump keeps it briefly) — that's what powers the special moves above. Just running isn't enough.

Hold up/down as you fire Special to aim it diagonally (straight by default).

Falling past the stage edge grabs the ledge — then jump to hop up, hold toward the stage to climb, or down to drop.
